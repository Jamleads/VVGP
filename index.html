About Vilavita Global Properties

Introduction

VilaVita Global Properties Limited (VVGP) is a reputable real estate development company with its head office in Abuja, FCT. We are registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria and dedicated to providing bespoke and top-notch services in the property market.

Our Mission

We work to create value for our stakeholders and clients by facilitating growth and development of communities, promoting home ownership, deployment of eastern and western technologies as well as innovative solutions that bring peace and happiness to our clients.

Our Vision

Marshal our resources and networks to promote home ownership, thriving and sustainable communities, utilizing modern technologies to deliver environmentally friendly dwelling places through responsible real estate practices.
